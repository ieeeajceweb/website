About us

IEEE AJCE Student Branch has been vibrant in the Kerala Section for more than a decade. The Student Branch consists of 150 plus active IEEE Student and Professional Membership holders. Under the umbrella of the Student Branch, two societies has been formed so that the members stay technically current, network with colleagues, and collaborate on research and projects, all while taking advantage of specialized opportunities.
